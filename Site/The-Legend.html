The Beginning

In the beginning there were the Universities. They were old, they were full of old people, and the young programmers suffered there.

Then came the private schools. They taught the young students, for a price. They had to suffer the pain of learning for years, and then years more would they need to offer they lives for the companies. It was better, for there was education, but it was still painful for the young programmers.

Then came a single young student, who was not like the others. He was determined there had to be a better way, and he decided to make it. He was named Nakov, and He was sent to free the young programmers from their suffering, and make them into developers. And so began the Legend of Nakov, and the Creation of all that was good for the young Developers.

The Ninja Academy

He had a dream. And the biggest part of that dream began when He created the Academy for Ninja Developers. There, he sought to train the young Developers to be more than developers, more than code monkeys. He sought to make them into Ninja Developers, wielding the dark arts of ASP.NET, Javascript, and many, many more. He began implanting his secret techniques in the minds of the young developers, and He saw that it was good.

But many of the young programmers gave up - the training was gruelling, and only a select few could finish it, and become true Ninja Developers. So He took the best of them under his wing, and He made them into Ninja Trainers, capable of passing his techniques and knowledge to the young programmers.

But that was still not enough, and so He left his Ninja Academy, and embarked on a journey to seek the proper path in order to create a true place of higher learning, where the young programmers could learn to be true Developers.

The University

Nakov had travelled far and wide, and He had created a plan. He banded together with his fellow trainers, young minds sharing His dream, and They created their utopia. It was called GagUni, and it was the place for the young programmers to live, learn, bond, drink and joke all day. It was, and is, the Answer He arrived to after his long years of travel.

And then came His disciples, who had taken His ideals to heart, and who wished to further better His dream.

And so it began, the Legend of GagUni!
